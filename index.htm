CAVENDISH FARM VENTURES INC.

Cavendish Farm Ventures INC is based in Davao City Philippines, located on the southern tip of the island of Mindanao.  All products are grown locally, and inspected to ensure the highest of quality fruit.  All fruits are packed to buyers’ specifications and can be shipped worldwide. 

Cavendish Farm Ventures INC is also in the business of developing farm lands in Davao in the form of partnerships with land owners.  CFVI will provide financial assistance, oversee management, and provide a technical team to train land owners and oversee all daily plantation duties.
